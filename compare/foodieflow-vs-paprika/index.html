FoodieFlow vs Paprika — FAQ

Is FoodieFlow a good alternative to Paprika?

It depends on what you want. Paprika is a recipe organiser you fill yourself. FoodieFlow is a meal-planning assistant that generates ideas, learns your dietary preferences, and plans around your pantry — a better fit if you want the app to do the thinking.

Does FoodieFlow let me save my own recipes like Paprika?

Yes. You can save any meal you love into your personal Meal Library, and the Smart Assistant uses it to suggest meals that suit your household.

Is FoodieFlow one-time or subscription?

FoodieFlow is free to download, with an optional Pro subscription from £3.99/month that unlocks unlimited Smart Assistant suggestions and full nutrition data.
