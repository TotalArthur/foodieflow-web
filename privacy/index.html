Privacy Policy.

How FoodieFlow collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Last updated: March 2026

In plain terms

FoodieFlow is designed to help you organise meals and plan groceries. To provide this service, we store the meals you save, the plans you create, and the account information needed to run the app.

We do not sell your data and we only collect information required to operate the service. You remain in control of your data and can request deletion at any time.

About this policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how FoodieFlow collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when you use the FoodieFlow mobile application and related services.

FoodieFlow is an independent software product developed and operated by its creator. This policy applies to the FoodieFlow mobile application and related services.

FoodieFlow is designed to help users organise meals, generate meal plans, and create shopping lists. To provide these features, we collect and process limited information necessary for the service to function.

If you do not agree with this policy, you should discontinue use of the service.

Information we collect

Account Information

When you create a FoodieFlow account, we collect:

  • Your email address, used to identify your account and send service-related communications
  • A display name or username, if you choose to provide one
  • Authentication credentials managed securely on your behalf

We do not collect your full legal name, postal address, or phone number as part of the standard registration process.

Meal and Dietary Data

FoodieFlow stores the information necessary to provide meal planning features, including:

  • Meals saved to your personal recipe library
  • Weekly meal plans generated or created by you
  • Shopping lists generated from your meal plans
  • Dietary preferences, allergies, and ingredient restrictions you choose to provide
  • Ingredients you indicate you have available at home
  • Optional AI-generated meal ideas requested within the app

This information is used solely to personalise your experience and deliver meal suggestions relevant to your preferences. It is never used for advertising or shared with third parties.

App Usage and Technical Data

We may collect limited technical information necessary to maintain and improve the service, including:

  • Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Crash reports and error logs, used solely for debugging and stability improvements
  • General usage patterns collected in aggregate and not linked to your identity
How we use your data

Service Delivery

Your personal data is used to:

  • Provide and operate the FoodieFlow application
  • Generate personalised meal suggestions based on your stated preferences and dietary requirements
  • Maintain your meal library, meal plans, and shopping lists across devices
  • Remember your settings and preferences between sessions

Communications

We may use your email address to send essential service notifications, respond to support requests you initiate, and notify you of significant changes to the app or these policies. We do not send marketing emails without your explicit consent, and you can opt out at any time.

AI Features

FoodieFlow may provide AI-generated meal ideas as part of its core features. When you request an AI meal suggestion, the ingredients or preferences you provide may be sent to an AI provider solely to generate the requested response.

This data is not used by FoodieFlow to train AI models and is not retained longer than necessary to deliver the feature.

Subscriptions and payments

Payment Handling

All subscription purchases and billing are handled entirely by Apple (via the App Store) or Google (via Google Play), depending on the platform you use. FoodieFlow does not collect, store, or process your payment card details, billing address, or any financial information. We receive only a confirmation of your subscription status in order to unlock premium features within the app.

Data storage and security

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using modern cloud infrastructure with encryption in transit and at rest. Access to user data is restricted and protected through authentication controls.

FoodieFlow is designed so that users can only access their own account data. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal information from loss, misuse, or unauthorised access.

While we implement strong safeguards, no system is entirely immune to risk. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your FoodieFlow account.

Service Providers

FoodieFlow relies on trusted infrastructure providers to operate the service, including:

  • Google Firebase for secure authentication and cloud database storage
  • Google Cloud for secure hosting and infrastructure

These providers process data only on our behalf and under strict data protection agreements. They do not use your data for their own purposes.

Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account remains active. If you request account deletion, we will permanently delete your personal data within 30 days of your request. Some anonymised aggregate data may be retained to help improve the service.

Account deletion

Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time by contacting us at support@foodieflow.app. When an account is deleted:

  • Your personal account information is permanently removed
  • Your saved meals, meal plans, and shopping lists are deleted
  • Any remaining personal data is removed from our systems within a reasonable timeframe

Some anonymised aggregate data, not linked to any individual, may be retained to help improve the service.

Your rights

Your Rights Under GDPR and Applicable Law

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — correct any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Erasure — request the deletion of your personal data
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to certain types of processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@foodieflow.app. We will respond within 30 days.

Third parties and children

We Do Not Sell Your Data

FoodieFlow does not sell, rent, trade, or monetise personal data. We do not partner with advertising networks, data brokers, or marketing platforms. Your information is used only to operate and improve the FoodieFlow service.

We may share data only where strictly necessary to operate the service — for example, with infrastructure providers that process data on our behalf under strict data processing agreements — and only for the purpose of delivering FoodieFlow to you.

Children's Privacy

FoodieFlow is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us at support@foodieflow.app and we will promptly remove the account and associated data.

Changes and contact

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date. If changes are material, we will notify you within the app or by email. Continued use of FoodieFlow after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at support@foodieflow.app. We aim to respond to all privacy-related enquiries within 48 hours on business days.
