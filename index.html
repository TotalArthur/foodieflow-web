How it works

From fridge to table in four steps.

Getting started takes minutes. Everything else happens automatically.

1

Set up your profile

Add your dietary preferences, allergies, and anything you want to avoid. Takes two minutes.

2

Generate meal ideas

Tap Generate and FoodieFlow produces personalised meal suggestions based on your preferences and what you have at home.

3

Plan your week

Drop meals into your weekly planner. Mix Foodie Assistant suggestions with meals from your personal library.

4

Shop and cook

Your shopping list is automatically generated from your plan, grouped by aisle. Walk in prepared.
