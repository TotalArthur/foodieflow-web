What FoodieFlow does

Everything you need to eat well, every week.

One app for your entire meal planning routine — from saving recipes to walking out of the supermarket.

Smart Meal Generator

Tell FoodieFlow your ingredients, dietary preferences, and goals — it generates personalised meal ideas instantly.

Weekly Planner

Fill your whole week in seconds. See every meal at a glance — breakfast, lunch, and dinner — and never scramble for ideas.

Smart Shopping Lists

FoodieFlow automatically builds your shopping list from your weekly plan, grouped by grocery aisle.

Your Meal Library

Save any meal you love. Build a collection of go-to recipes tailored to your household — and generate new suggestions based on them.

Dietary Preferences

Set allergies, dietary restrictions, and disliked ingredients once. FoodieFlow remembers them every time.

Nutrition Tracking

See macro and calorie estimates for every meal. Pro users get full detailed breakdowns for every recipe.

How it works

From fridge to table in four steps.

1

Set up your profile

Add your dietary preferences, allergies, and anything you want to avoid. Takes two minutes.

2

Generate meal ideas

Tap Generate and FoodieFlow produces a personalised set of meal suggestions based on your preferences and what you have at home.

3

Plan your week

Drop meals into your weekly planner. Mix AI suggestions with meals from your personal library.

4

Shop and cook

Your shopping list is automatically generated from your plan, grouped by aisle. Walk in prepared.
